]]> By: Jenna https://budgetsaresexy.vercel.app/spending-more-to-save-more/#comment-56133 Tue, 28 Jun 2011 17:06:36 +0000 https://staging.budgetsaresexy.vercel.app/?p=11147#comment-56133 I think this has gotten most of us at one time or another. I know a lot of people who get a little Groupon-crazy and end up trying to sell them off later when they realize they’ve overspent!

As you mention, the key is knowing your budget at the outset, and then pausing for just a couple seconds before you buy to figure out if that extra cost is in your budget. Once you get in the habit, it’s not as hard to pass deals up.
