]]> By: Joe https://budgetsaresexy.vercel.app/5-a-day-grants-you-millionaire-status/#comment-238559 Mon, 11 Jul 2016 14:18:59 +0000 https://staging.budgetsaresexy.vercel.app/?p=1081#comment-238559 So over the past few weeks I have been thinking about the best solution to save money for the future. I am 36yrs old, not married, and no kids to speak of. Up until now, I haven’t been able to save a penny.

Now the $150/month might sound like a lot of money, but here is what I did. My job allows me to add a deduction on my paycheck, as most jobs will. So in doing so, I was able to set up a ACH Direct Deposit to another bank account for $35/week; this will come out of the paycheck and whatever is left will get Direct Deposited into my regular account.

$35/wk = $150/mo = $1800/yr @ 5yrs = $9000/saved!
