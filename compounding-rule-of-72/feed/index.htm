If you assume that inflation runs at 3% per year, 72 divided by 3 equal 24, meaning cost of living will double about every 24 years. So while you may think you can live off a certain income level now, remember it will take DOUBLE that to live the same level of lushness a quarter of a century from now.

Depressing math, I know, but very helpful when doing projections of what one is going to need at retirement.
