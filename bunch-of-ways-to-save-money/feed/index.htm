It’s a good way of investing more money in little bits – I’ve been doing this with my contributions to 2 retirement accounts for the last year.

I increase the amount each month by +1% of the previous month’s sum. This is the bit which throws colleagues when we ever talk about it because they say that 1% isn’t worth doing.

As I find this too fiddly to manually adjust each month I just add 5 bucks each month until the next higher amount is crossed and needs adding another 5 bucks. In reality this means that I only had to adjust my contribution every 3 or 4 months.
