]]> By: Manuel Schulze https://budgetsaresexy.vercel.app/great-money-ideas-to-steal/#comment-251730 Wed, 29 Mar 2017 10:55:55 +0000 https://staging.budgetsaresexy.vercel.app/?p=50869#comment-251730 One game changer for me was the 31 days of financial independence guide of Trent Hamm from TheSimpleDollar.com.
