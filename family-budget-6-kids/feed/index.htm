The same story rings true for doctors, but there is a family health clinic that’s $60-110 per visit base. I just don’t see being able to manipulate what you do to fit our family, even though we both have 6 kids. Crazy how different life is in different parts of the US. We do have some debts that you don’t have, so that could be part of it as well. I have college loans we pay on even though I don’t work. We pay $2000/month for our 1900sqft home. Your post has given me great insight in some areas, while baffling me in others.
