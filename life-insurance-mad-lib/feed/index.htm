I currently pay roughly $30 a month for it through a Dutch insurance company, and I am relatively not healthy. If I had one piece of advice to give to anyone shopping for life insurance today, it would be to buy term life insurance that lasts long enough for your dependents to become independent, OR long enough for you to become wealthy – whichever comes first.
