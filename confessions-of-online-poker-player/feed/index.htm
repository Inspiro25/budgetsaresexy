This post was obviously written before Black Friday, and now the only sites available to Americans are basically filled with sharks. But the point I want to make is a lot of it is timing; the guy I know who made the $250k cash built his bankroll on $100 and $200 sngs, playing 8-12 at a time. He was a college freshman during the “golden age” of poker where ppl really didn’t know what they were doing, so he could make a high profit margin on these games. Today even the best players only make a few % profit margin, and it varies greatly.

I personally only play now every once in a while for fun. Yes, I try to win, but I am not going to put in the hours to build a bankroll and try to make consistent side income doing it. I do hope to play in the WSOP some day, but probably in a lesser event (i.e. $1.5k event vs. $10k main event). I still love to see what’s going on with poker and the culture surrounding it.
