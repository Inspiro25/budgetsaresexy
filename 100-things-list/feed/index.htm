]]> By: Torrie @ To Love and To Learn https://budgetsaresexy.vercel.app/100-things-list/#comment-277697 Mon, 29 Apr 2019 14:46:57 +0000 https://staging.budgetsaresexy.vercel.app/?p=47387#comment-277697 I made a 101 in 1001 list, which is *kind of* similar, in that you set up 101 goals/dreams for yourself that you want to accomplish over the next 1,001 days (which is about 2.75 years). What I like about it is that it gives a deadline, which my personality type totally needs :)

To get to 101 things, I found it was easiest for me to set up categories (books, finances, family life, travel, etc.), and then I brainstormed several ideas under each. Some things were small and easily accomplished (open up savings accounts for the kids), and others are huge and taking months and months (get to 100% fluency in French on Duolingo).

I love reading other people’s dream lists, so make sure you post the whole list when you have it!
