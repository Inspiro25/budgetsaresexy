]]> By: WorkingMom https://budgetsaresexy.vercel.app/side-hustle-tv-film-extra-background-actor/#comment-292945 Wed, 15 Jan 2020 14:51:55 +0000 https://staging.budgetsaresexy.vercel.app/?p=62347#comment-292945 Just an encouragement that even if you aren’t in a big city or an area where films are shot, there could be some local opportunities. I work for a restaurant chain and we are shooting ads almost every month. We’ve exhausted our “friends and family” we can convince to be extras and I’ve taken to just using facebook to see who is interested and available. If you’re interested, I’d find some local talent agents (you’ll get paid more if you go through an agent) or you could just post on some local sites that you’re interested in doing that kind of work/contact some local advertising agencies. A few good headshots with different looks really sets you apart! We are especially always looking for people who are available during the week day (families who home school or those with flexible work schedules) and people of diverse ethnic backgrounds (we live in a very non-diverse area but want our ads to better reflect the makeup of our country, not just the town where we live). My kids were just extras in an ad this week and got paid in restaurant gift cards but they thought it was fun!
