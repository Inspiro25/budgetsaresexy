]]> By: Scott https://budgetsaresexy.vercel.app/junk-silver-coin-collecting-check-your-pockets/#comment-108180 Wed, 12 Sep 2012 13:12:28 +0000 https://staging.budgetsaresexy.vercel.app/?p=27643#comment-108180 @J Money – you really want to avoid grading unless you have a nice coin (generally several $100 in value). The cost to get a coin slabbed is generally $20-50 with all fees, so you dont want to put a $50 coin in a $30 slab – in resale you’ll instantly lose any slabbing costs – if you’re interested in graded/slabbed stuff, it’s always better to buy it already graded/slabbed – let the other person take a financial bath on the fees ;)

If you’re just looking in terms of silver value, third party grading doesnt offer any real value – it’s really for nice collectible coins ;)
