That way you have a ‘plan’. You continue to pay for repairs and pay down mortgage and whatever while your savings remain at that level. If it dips below that you stop the mortgage plan and throw everything back in savings until you get back to your original 70K. This way you have a PLAN. When x happens you do y. If you push thru and pay off the smaller mortgage than that is a lot of cash flow you free up and will be amazing on its own.
